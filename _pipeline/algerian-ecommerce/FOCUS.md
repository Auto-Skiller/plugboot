# algerian_ecommerce — Focus Brief

## Overview
Algerian e-commerce business focus. Serve Algerian e-commerce sellers.

## Key Context
- **Market:** Algeria — not Shopify/Western. Local platforms, cash-on-delivery dominant
- **Currency:** DZD (Dinar Algérien) — always price in DZD
- **Delivery:** Local delivery services are key infrastructure
- **Advertising:** Facebook Ads is the primary marketing channel in Algeria, Tiktok
- **Payment:** Cash on delivery (COD) is dominant, limited card/online payment adoption
- **Language:** Arabic/Darija for customers, French for business communication
- **Product strategy:** Think about what Algerian sellers need as tools
