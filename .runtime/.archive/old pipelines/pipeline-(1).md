# PLAYBOOK: The Agentic Workflow


## 1. Phase Definitions

| Phase | Category | Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **01-04** | **Strategy & Definition** | Ideation, Research, Analysis, and Planning. |
| **05-06** | **Foundations** | Infrastructure, Architecture, UX, and Design. |
| **07-08** | **Build & Production** | Coding, Media Production, Assets, and Copy. |
| **09-11** | **Quality & Validation** | Testing, Security, Post-Production, and Staging. |
| **12-13** | **Launch & Growth** | Deployment, Distribution, Marketing, and Sales. |
| **14** | **Sustainability** | Maintenance, Support, and Long-term Analytics. |
