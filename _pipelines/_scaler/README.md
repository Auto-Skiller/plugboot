# ⚖️ Scaler Pipeline: System Assimilation & Evolution

## Objective
The Scaler is the primary metabolic engine of the **Agentic OS v5**. Its purpose is to ingest external discoveries (tools, skills, logic, business strategies) and internal gaps, then strategically architect and integrate them into the workspace while preserving the foundational OS DNA.

---

## 🏛️ Pillar-Based Architecture
The pipeline is organized into three distinct pillars of evolution:

1.  **`_Foundational_Integrity`** (Stability): Core OS systems, identity laws, routing schemes, and structural blueprints.
2.  **`_Operational_Muscles`** (Power): The `.toolbox_library`, automation scripts, agentic skills, and capability engineering.
3.  **`_Value_Generation`** (Growth): Monetization logic, business strategies, and value-driven market deployments.

---

## 🧠 Strategic Intelligence (v3.1)
The Scaler operates using a high-fidelity **Strategic Interrogation** engine:

*   **Double-Scan Protocol**: Before proposing any change, the Scaler performs a `Source Scan` (Discovery maturity) and a `Target Scan` (Ground Truth via Meta-Routers).
*   **Integration Resolution**: Actions are dynamically resolved into four strategic types:
    *   **INJECT**: Append missing logic to existing files.
    *   **UPGRADE**: Modernize existing components while preserving foundational DNA.
    *   **BUILD_NEW**: Create and register entirely new components in the correct routers.
    *   **EXTEND**: Add sibling features via cohesive expansion patterns.
*   **DNA Preservation Law**: Mandates modular merging and parity audits to ensure the system evolves without losing its core identity.

---

## 🚦 Operational Flow
1.  **Discovery**: External items land in `.mixed_inbox/` or pillar inboxes.
2.  **Classification**: Items are routed to the correct Pillar via **Utility-First Routing**.
3.  **Mapping (v3.1)**: Perform Double-Scan and Strategic Interrogation to identify targets.
4.  **Proposing**: Generate **Proposal Cards (v3.1)** in the relevant `. [Pillar]_proposals/` folder.
5.  **Integration**: Execute changes, perform **Verification Scans**, and trigger `.sync_engine`.

---

## 📂 Stucture Overview
*   **`EXTERNAL/`**: Active discoveries and proposals organized by pillar.
*   **`INTERNAL/`**: System gaps and internal solution cards.
*   **`.scaler.meta/`**: Isolated operational logic (Runbooks, Trackers, Scratchpad).
