## 📜 Commercial Status Explanation

### What You Have: Utility Registration as "Commerce"

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Water Company (SEAAL/ADE)** | Registered as "commerce" – pays commercial rates |
| **Former Use** | Medical practice (cabinet médical) |
| **Current Status** | Property can potentially be used commercially |

> [!NOTE]
> **Utility Registration ≠ Registre de Commerce**
> - Being registered as "commerce" with utilities means the property WAS used commercially
> - The actual **Registre de Commerce** (business license) was in your father's name (الله يرحمه)
> - A new tenant wanting to run a business would need to apply for their OWN Registre de Commerce

### What This Means for Rent Value

| Scenario | Value Impact |
|----------|--------------|
| **Commercial utility history** | +15% to +25% (shows the property can be used commercially) |
| **If you obtain/transfer Registre** | +40% to +50% (proven legal commercial use) |
| **Standard residential** | Base rate |

### Options to Verify/Obtain Commercial License

1. **Check with Commune (APC Mohammadia)** – Ask if a Registre de Commerce exists for this address
2. **Check with your family documents** – Your father's practice documents may include the Registre
3. **New tenant can apply** – If they want commercial use, they can apply for their own Registre using this address

#### If Renting as Commercial (15% IRG)

| Gross Rent | Tax (15%) | Net Income |
|------------|-----------|------------|
| 90,000 DZD | 13,500 DZD | **76,500 DZD** |
| 100,000 DZD | 15,000 DZD | **85,000 DZD** |
| 115,000 DZD | 17,250 DZD | **97,750 DZD** |
| 130,000 DZD | 19,500 DZD | **110,500 DZD** |

### Tax Payment Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **When to pay** | Within first 20 days of each month following receipt of rent |
| **Where to pay** | Tax office (Direction des Impôts) of the property location |
| **Declaration** | Monthly declaration required |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Commercial rent pays 15% tax** but commands higher rent. Even after the 15% IRG:
> - Commercial rent of 100,000 DZD → Net 85,000 DZD
> - Residential rent of 85,000 DZD → Net 79,050 DZD
> 
> **Commercial is still more profitable!**
