
## Quick Start

### For Human Operators
1. Set strategic goals in `board.yaml` (See `08-Board Guide.md`)
2. Switch to appropriate mode (STRICT/COLLAB/AUTO)
3. Let agents execute autonomously

### For AI Agents
1. **Read `AGENTS.md`** — Understand your persona, operational modes, and behavioral protocols, Core Capabilities, workspace structure and other resources
2. **Check `board.yaml`** — Detect current mode and active goals (See `08-Board Guide.md`)
3. **Check `index.yaml`** — Identify relevant Domains and departments, Agents & Skills
