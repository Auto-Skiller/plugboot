
### For Human Operators
1. Set strategic goals in `board.yaml` (See `Board Guide.md`)
2. Switch to appropriate mode (NORMAL/COLLAB/AUTO)
3. Let agents execute autonomously

### For AI Agents
1. **Read `AGENTS.md`** - Understand your persona, operational modes, and behavioral protocols, Core Capabilities, workspace structure and others resources
2. **Check `board.yaml`** - Detect current mode and active goals (See `Board Guide.md`)
3. **Check `index.yaml`** - Identify Relevents Domains and departments , Agents & Skills

### 2. Usage Protocol
1. **Identify**: Check `index.yaml` to find the relevant Domain/Department.
2. **Consult**: Read the `{department}.yaml` guide to understand available resources.
3. **Execute**: Load the necessary rules, knowledge, and scripts to perform the task.
